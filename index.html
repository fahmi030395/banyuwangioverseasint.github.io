About Us

PT. Banyuwangi Overseas International is a highly motivated company that eager to face new challenge to deliver our customer the best quality products, as our main priority to make sure our customer satisfaction.

We buy raw materials from Konjac farmers spreads across Indonesia as our main mission to help Konjac farmers earn a worthy income for their family. Moreover, we also seek to create and promote healthier lifestyle. We strive to grow our business with the same honesty and integrity we use to deliver our products with sustainability and great quality for all.
